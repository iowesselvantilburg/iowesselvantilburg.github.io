Introduction

The landal app is a mobile app that shows the bookings a guest has made. It also shows the guest the information about the park and the accommodation. The app is available for iOS and Android.

Main features​

  • use your app as a key to open your accommodation
  • prepare your holiday in the app by providing your travel companions and more info to Landal
  • view your bookings
  • view your holiday park information
  • view your holiday park map
  • check in and check out of your stay
  • view the parks activities, facilities and restaurants
  • we got some fun festivity animations and minigames in the app, built using react-native-skia
  • much more...
